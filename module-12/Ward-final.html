Hobbies

Basketball

These are the basketball courts of Pioneer Park in Kankakee, Illinois where I can honestly say I have faced some of the best basketball talent in my life. Pioneer Park became one of those courts that if you're not good enough then you'll never see the main court. It was a hierarchy or sorts, but every young basketball player from this town wanted to get the chance to play on what is considered the "main court". My love for basketball started at this place. I credit it for teaching me a significant amount of things outside of how to play the game.

My contract in the military was short, but as a veteran I recommend it to everyone who is curious. I am unsure if I would re-enlist at this very moment, but if I did I would re-enlist as an Airman again.
